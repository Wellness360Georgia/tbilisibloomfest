The festival

Two days. One blooming city.

Bloom Fest is Georgia's first premium platform where wellness, culture and creative industries share one roof. Inside the 85-year-old Tbilisi Balneological Spa Resort, seven spaces come alive with exhibitions, workshops, yoga, live music, a curated market of natural goods, and voices from the global wellness field.

Almost every resort in Georgia takes part. Local makers bring their best. International guests arrive to see what this country is becoming: a destination for natural regeneration, with mineral waters, healing traditions and a new generation of wellness culture.

What to expect

An experience curated across every sense.

01 Yoga & movement
Morning practice in the courtyard under the plane trees. Teachers from across the Caucasus.
02 Curated market
Natural Georgian cosmetics, handmade soap, eco goods, small producers you won't find on high streets.
03 Made by hand
Every product is the story of a maker. Every maker, the story of a place.
04 Art & installations
Curated exhibitions, installation performance, and a luxury collection that tells Georgia's mineral story.
05 Gardens & courtyards
Two walled gardens. Fountains, lanterns, quiet corners for conversation and rest.
06 Taste & reception
Georgian wines, natural waters, seasonal menus by local chefs.

The venue

85 years of healing under one roof.

The Tbilisi Balneological Spa Resort opened in 1938 and has drawn those seeking thermal cure ever since. Colonnaded halls, pendant lights the size of moons, walled gardens, a second-floor orangery. Every corner is part of the programme.
